ACME CORPORATION — EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK 2026
Internal Use Only

========================================
1. COMPANY OVERVIEW
========================================

Acme Corporation is a technology company headquartered in San Francisco. All employees are expected to follow company policies outlined in this handbook. For questions not covered here, contact HR at hr@acme.com or IT at helpdesk@acme.com.

========================================
2. WORKING HOURS AND REMOTE WORK
========================================

Standard working hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM local time. Employees may request remote work up to three days per week. Remote work must be approved by your direct manager at least 48 hours in advance. Full-time remote work requires approval from HR and your department head.

========================================
3. PAID TIME OFF AND VACATION
========================================

Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid vacation per calendar year. Vacation requests must be submitted in the HR portal at least two weeks before the start date. Managers must approve vacation requests within five business days. Unused vacation days may roll over up to five days into the next year. Vacation days expire on March 31 if not used.

========================================
4. SICK LEAVE
========================================

Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave does not roll over. For absences longer than three consecutive days, a doctor's note is required. Report sick leave to your manager before 10:00 AM on the first day of absence.

========================================
5. PASSWORD AND IT SECURITY
========================================

All employees must change their password every 90 days. Passwords must be at least 12 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. Do not share passwords or use the same password for personal accounts. Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) on all company systems. Report suspicious emails to security@acme.com immediately.

For password resets, use the self-service portal at password.acme.com or contact the IT helpdesk. IT helpdesk hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM Pacific Time.

========================================
6. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
========================================

Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of purchase through the Finance portal. Receipts are required for all expenses over $25. Meal expenses during business travel are reimbursed up to $75 per day. Personal expenses will not be reimbursed. Expense reports are processed within 10 business days after manager approval.

========================================
7. ONBOARDING FOR NEW HIRES
========================================

New hires receive a welcome email on their first day with login credentials and equipment pickup instructions. Onboarding includes a company orientation on day one, IT setup on day two, and a meeting with HR to review benefits. New employees are assigned a buddy for their first 30 days. Complete all mandatory compliance training within the first two weeks.

========================================
8. BENEFITS ENROLLMENT
========================================

Health insurance enrollment opens during the first 30 days of employment. Employees can choose between three medical plans: Basic, Standard, and Premium. Dental and vision coverage are included in the Standard and Premium plans. Benefits questions should be directed to benefits@acme.com.

========================================
9. CODE OF CONDUCT
========================================

Acme maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, discrimination, and workplace violence. Violations should be reported to HR or through the anonymous ethics hotline at 1-800-555-0199. Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited.

========================================
10. EQUIPMENT AND LAPTOP POLICY
========================================

Company laptops remain the property of Acme Corporation. Employees must return all equipment within five business days of their last working day. Lost or stolen laptops must be reported to IT within 24 hours. Personal software may not be installed on company devices without IT approval.
